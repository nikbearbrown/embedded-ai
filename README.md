## About the Author

**Nik Bear Brown** teaches AI, data science, and computational thinking at Northeastern University. He is the architect of the **Brutalist** AI-assisted production system, whose renderer-agnostic architecture is designed to operate under constraints not unlike the embedded-systems constraints this book covers.

He is the founder of [Humanitarians AI](https://www.humanitarians.ai/), the author of the *with LLMs* textbook series, and the author of *Computational Skepticism for AI*. His PhD in Computer Science is from UCLA.

He works in Boston. [nikbearbrown.com](https://www.nikbearbrown.com) · [irreducibly.xyz](https://irreducibly.xyz) · [skepticism.ai](https://www.skepticism.ai)
