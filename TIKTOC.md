# Embedded AI
## Compact Tik TOC Architecture

**Book type:** Technical practitioner textbook  
**Status:** Retrospective architecture for developed content  

## Core Thesis

This book teaches embedded AI as the practice of deploying intelligent behavior under physical constraints: power, latency, memory, sensors, reliability, safety, and maintainability.

## Primary Reader

Engineering students, makers, embedded developers, robotics learners, and applied AI practitioners moving from notebooks to devices.

## Learning Arc

1. **Embedded constraints:** hardware, sensors, timing, memory, power, and deployment targets.
2. **Model-to-device pipeline:** data, feature extraction, model selection, quantization, testing, and integration.
3. **Field reliability:** monitoring, safety, updates, debugging, and human-centered deployment.

## Chapter Architecture

Each chapter should begin with a device behavior, identify the constraint, then show the design tradeoff and verification method.

## Terminal Capability

The reader can design and evaluate a small embedded AI system that respects hardware constraints and real-world reliability demands.

## Adoption Risks

- Hardware examples age quickly.
- AI model details can overwhelm embedded fundamentals.
- Safety and testing must not be left until the end.

## Production Notes

Use Tik TOC to align the manuscript around a build arc from sensor to deployed behavior.
