Figure 9.5 — Split inference: cut where the activation is small

MobileNetV2 with 224×224×3 input. Blocks 1–6 on device, blocks 7–13 plus head on cloud. The decision is the bandwidth check, not the layer count.

Activation tensors shrink toward the middle of the network. The split-cut goes where they are smallest — usually well before the halfway mark of the parameter count.
