Figure 7.3 — Race to sleep

Same model, two clocks. Faster clock means shorter active window means lower average current.

Trace A (64 MHz, 20 mA for 400 ms) and Trace B (128 MHz, 35 mA for 200 ms) both wake every 10 s. The taller, shorter burst integrates to less energy because sleep is so much cheaper than slowed-down activity.
